# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date**: February 17, 2026

## 1. Data Collection

Mount0 is a local virtual filesystem library. It does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on our servers. All data processing happens locally on your machine.

## 2. Website Analytics

Our website may use privacy-preserving analytics to understand visitor trends. No personally identifiable information (PII) is collected.

## 3. Third-Party Services

If you use Mount0 with third-party storages (like S3), their respective privacy policies apply to the data stored there.

## 4. Contact

If you have questions about our privacy practices, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
